You’ve arrived to PARACOSM TAROT

A fun game where you can get an inside on your past, present, future or a certain situation.

All you need to do is to think of a question.

(ex. What do I need to know now? How is my love life going to be? Will my skin ever turn purple?)

Whatever you want to know or whatever your intention is - tune into the energy of that question and feel which one of the cards resonates with it the most.

Once you’ve picked your card - click on it and see what it says.

Remember that you can interpret your card however you like.

Maybe the colors are telling you something - or maybe it’s the item on the picture that reminds you of something - every way of reading it is right!
