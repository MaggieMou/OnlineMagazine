Self care is not selfish

The autumn is slowly kicking in and soon there won't be any leaves on trees left.

You may have gotten carried away working on something too much.

Deadlines are important but you shouldn't sacrifise your well-being in order to meet them.

Hard work is kind of overrated anyway.

Do something that you love to be doing, take care, pop some vitamins...

And your work will benefit from it too!
